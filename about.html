About Us

WE SAVE CHILDREN'S LIVES. WE FIGHT FOR THEIR RIGHTS.

We help children beat deadly illness like pneumonia and diarrhoea. We make sure they get the food they need to survive and grow up healthy. When disasters strike, we're there saving lives. The right to life, to food, to clean water and protection is provided. We campaign for free school meals. Lots of children are benifited from this. We work in India, helping more children get ready for kindergarten than any other non-profit organizations. We work in the hardest to reach places where it's toughest to be a child.
